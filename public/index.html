FAQ

Is this just another dating app?

No. TrueMatch is curated. You receive a shortlist, then approve or pass. When there is mutual interest, we help move things forward so you are not stuck in never-ending small talk.

How do I get matches?

You set preferences, we curate profiles into your shortlist, and you decide.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. You can cancel anytime. Your access continues until the end of your billing cycle.
